How Jimothy became a legend

Every hero has an origin. His just happens to be a dumpster behind a taco stand.

Born in the bins

Jimothy came into the world with a short spine — hunched, stubby, impossible to ignore. The others said he would never make it past the recycling. Jimothy just started walking.

02

The big adventure

From the alley to the forest to the blockchain, Jimothy waddles onward — collecting shiny coins, dodging the Catcher’s hounds, and proving a short spine can carry a giant heart.
